# Spell Weaver

Spell Weavers are artificers with a particular interest in the enchantment of delicate materials.
They weave arcane energy into fabric and control ephemeral threads to aid their rudimentary martial abilities.
Experienced Spell Weavers can infuse robes and sets of cloth with magic,
that other artificers could only apply to armor.

## Subclass Features

### level 3

#### Tool Proficiency

When you adopt this specialization at 3rd level, you gain proficiency with weaver's tools.
Your magic can take the form of threads and needles which can stand-in as weaver's tools.

#### Spell Woven Armor

When you reach 3rd level, you gain the ability to weave magical threads into a translucent armor.
You can perform a 10-minute ritual to progressively build up these threads around your body.
The spell woven armor persists for 24 hours and adds your Intelligence modifier to you armor class.
You can not benefit from this bonus while wearing any armor, using a shield
or adding your Intelligence modifier to your armor class by other means.

#### Guiding Threads

You command magical, translucent threads that support your control over martial weapons.

When you reach 3rd level, you gain proficiency with martial weapons.
A Melee Weapon that has the versatile property, has the finesse property when you wield it.

Immediately after attacking with a thrown weapon, you can pull on your magical threads to return it to your hand.

### level 5

#### Accelerating Threads

Starting at 5th level, you can add your Intelligence modifier (minimum of +1) to the damage you deal with any Melee Weapon that you
wield with two hands.

#### Infusion Weaving

Starting at 5th level, you gain the ability to infuse your clothing, as if it were armor.
Infusions on clothes are active only while you are wearing them.

You can apply the following infusions to clothing:

- Armor of Magical Strength
- Enhanced Defense
- Mind Sharpener
- Resistant Armor

### level 9

#### Magical Evasion

Starting at 9th level, you learned to pull yourself out of harms way using your magical threads.
When you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage,
you instead take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, and only half damage if you fail.
